## Overview

**Nexora Island** is a sovereign innovation nation established in 2026, dedicated to technological progress, digital governance, and global economic leadership. This repository hosts the official government web portal — a fully responsive, single-page application that provides citizens and visitors with information about the nation's government, smart cities, visa processes, corporate charter, and more.

The portal is designed to represent Nexora Island on the world stage: professional, accessible, and built with modern web standards.

## Features

- **Responsive Design** — Fully optimised for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports using Bootstrap 5.
- **Smooth Animations** — Scroll-triggered entrance animations powered by AOS (Animate On Scroll).
- **Interactive Island Map** — Dedicated SVG-based map page with clickable city and infrastructure points, filter controls, and a sidebar info panel.
- **Accessible Markup** — ARIA labels, roles, semantic HTML, and keyboard-navigable map points throughout.
- **SEO Optimised** — Complete Open Graph, Twitter Card, structured data (Schema.org), and canonical URL support.
- **National Identity Showcase** — Animated national flag and government emblem rendered in pure CSS.
- **Digital Governance Sections** — Government structure, ministries, and national institutions presented clearly.
- **Visa Information** — Comprehensive Visa on Arrival eligibility, requirements, and duration details.
- **Corporate Charter** — Downloadable PDF of the official Nexora Corporation founding document.
- **Scroll-to-Top Button** — Floating, animated scroll helper for long-page navigation.
- **Stats Bar** — Animated counter displaying key national statistics on load.

## Visa on Arrival

International visitors may enter Nexora Island through the streamlined **Visa on Arrival** programme.

### Eligibility
Citizens of most countries across Africa, the Americas, Asia-Pacific, Europe, the Middle East, and South Asia are eligible.

### Entry Points
- Nexora International Airport
- Nexora International Port

### Requirements
1. Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity beyond departure date)
2. Return or onward ticket
3. Accommodation confirmation (hotel booking or official invitation letter)
4. Visa processing fee: **50 NXC** (payable at point of entry)

### Duration
- Standard stay: **up to 30 days**
- Extension: up to 30 additional days · fee: **30 NXC**

📧 Contact: [immigration@nexora.gov](mailto:immigration@nexora.gov)
