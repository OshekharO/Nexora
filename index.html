About Nexora Island

Nexora Island is a sovereign innovation ecosystem created to support advanced technology development, digital governance, and global economic collaboration.

The island operates under the Nexora Government and is supported by Nexora Corporation — the central organization responsible for infrastructure, economic development, and technological innovation.
