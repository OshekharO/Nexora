How We Govern

Government Structure

A modern administrative framework designed to support technological innovation, economic development, and national governance.

President of Nexora

Head of state and chief executive responsible for national leadership, international relations, and the strategic direction of Nexora Island.

Nexora Parliament

The legislative body responsible for passing national laws, economic policies, and technological regulations that shape the future of Nexora Island.

Ministry of Technology

Oversees digital infrastructure, AI development, cybersecurity, and national technology initiatives.

Ministry of Finance

Responsible for national economic policy, fiscal management, and regulation of the Nexora Credit (NXC) currency.

Ministry of Infrastructure

Manages transportation, ports, airports, energy infrastructure, and smart-city planning.

Nexora Central Bank

Supervises the Nexora Credit (NXC) currency and maintains national financial stability.

Nexora Immigration Authority

Manages border control, visas, and entry regulations for all international visitors.

Nexora Space & Research Agency

Leads aerospace research, satellite programs, and advanced scientific exploration.

Entry to Nexora

Visa on Arrival

International visitors may enter Nexora Island through our streamlined Visa on Arrival program.

Eligibility

Citizens of most countries may apply for a Visa on Arrival when entering Nexora Island through Nexora International Airport or Nexora International Port.

Most nationalities eligible
Requirements
1
Valid Passport Must be valid for the duration of stay
2
Return or Onward Ticket Proof of departure from Nexora
3
Accommodation Confirmation Hotel booking or invitation letter
4
Visa Processing Fee Payable at point of entry
Visa Duration

The standard Visa on Arrival allows visitors to remain in Nexora Island for up to 30 days, with optional extensions through the Nexora Immigration Office.
