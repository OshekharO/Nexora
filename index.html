About Nexora Island

Nexora Island is a sovereign innovation ecosystem dedicated to technological progress, digital governance, and global collaboration. The island operates under the Nexora Government and is supported by Nexora Corporation, responsible for infrastructure development, technology innovation, and economic growth.

Government Structure

The Government of Nexora Island operates through a modern administrative framework designed to support technological innovation, economic development, and national governance.

President of Nexora

Head of state and chief executive responsible for national leadership, international relations, and strategic direction of Nexora Island.

Nexora Parliament

The legislative body responsible for passing national laws, economic policies, and technological regulations.

Ministry of Technology

Oversees digital infrastructure, artificial intelligence development, cybersecurity, and national technology initiatives.

Ministry of Finance

Responsible for national economic policy, fiscal management, and regulation of the Nexora Credit (NXC) currency.

Ministry of Infrastructure

Manages transportation systems, ports, airports, energy infrastructure, and smart-city planning.

Nexora Central Bank

Supervises the Nexora Credit (NXC) currency and national financial stability.

Nexora Immigration Authority

Manages border control, visas, and entry regulations for international visitors.

Nexora Space & Research Agency

Leads aerospace research, satellite programs, and advanced scientific exploration.

Visa on Arrival

International visitors may enter Nexora Island through the Visa on Arrival program.

Eligibility

Citizens of most countries may apply for a Visa on Arrival when entering Nexora Island through Nexora International Airport or Nexora International Port.

Requirements
  • Valid passport
  • Return or onward ticket
  • Accommodation confirmation
  • Visa processing fee
Visa Duration

The standard Visa on Arrival allows visitors to remain in Nexora Island for up to 30 days with optional extensions through the Nexora Immigration Office.
