Visa on Arrival

International visitors may enter Nexora Island through the Visa on Arrival program.

Eligibility

Citizens of most countries may apply for a Visa on Arrival when entering Nexora Island through Nexora International Airport or Nexora International Port.

Requirements
  • Valid passport
  • Return or onward ticket
  • Accommodation confirmation
  • Visa processing fee
Visa Duration

The standard Visa on Arrival allows visitors to remain in Nexora Island for up to 30 days with optional extensions through the Nexora Immigration Office.
