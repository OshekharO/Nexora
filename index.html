About Nexora

Nexora Island is an independent technological ecosystem that blends sovereign governance with corporate innovation. The island operates under the Nexora Government and is supported by Nexora Corporation — a strategic entity responsible for infrastructure, technology development, and economic expansion.

Nexora aims to become a global center for advanced research, digital infrastructure, artificial intelligence, and future economies.
